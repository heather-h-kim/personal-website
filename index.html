About Me

As a Korean-English translator, when I first heard about coding and coding languages, I thought "I'm good at learning a new language, why not learning coding languages?" I indeed found similarity between linguistic languages and coding languages. Just like I became more fluent in English when I recognized patterns in English, I could understand new codes better when I noticed patterns and logic from the seemingly complex codes. I enjoy exploring new codes and trouble shooting using the patterns and logic I found!

When I'm not coding I like doing pilates and snuggling with my cat. She's so cute.

Kitty Weight Watcher

As a responsible cat owner, you need to make sure that your cat is being fed right amount of food so that the lovely fur baby can stay at a healthy weight. Kitty Weight Watcher is an app that calculates recommended daily calories and feeding amounts based on the information that the user entered. For those users who feed their cats wet food and dry food, this app lets the users choose the wet food vs dry food ratio so that it can suggest feeding amounts for each type of food.
